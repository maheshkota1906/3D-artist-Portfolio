About Me

I'm Durga naga mahesh, I'm passionate 3D artist with one year of hands-on experience in creating characters, props, and environments for games and animations. Proficient in Autodesk Maya for 3D modeling and Photoshop for texturing, I specialize in producing both stylized and realistic assets optimized for real-time applications.

My work includes developing detailed characters with proper topology, designing props that enhance storytelling, and building immersive environments that support gameplay and cinematic sequences. I also have experience in UV mapping, baking maps, and creating clean textures that match the artistic direction of a project. With a solid understanding of game-ready asset production and a creative eye for detail,I aim to contribute high-quality visuals that bring projects to life.
